Frequently asked questions

The admission process for both B.Ed and D.El.Ed courses will involve an online application form to be filled out and submitted with the required documents. Shortlisted candidates may be called for an interview or entrance examination. Selection criteria may include academic and professional background, course aptitude, communication skills, etc.

A. The eligibility criteria for a B.Ed is 50% in UG/PG for General/OBC & 45% in UG/PG for SC/ST. The age limit is 35 years for General and 40 years for SC/ST/PH.

For D.El.Ed course, 50% For GEN/OBC & 45% for SC/ST in 10+2 With an age limit of 35 years GEN, 38 years OBC, 40 years. SC/ST/PH.

Documents to be submitted are bachelor's degree/10+2 certificate and mark sheet per eligibility criteria, valid identity proof, address proof, recent passport-size Colored photographs, Cast certificates, and booking amount as stated in the prospectus.

A. The fee structure for B.Ed and D.El.Ed courses may differ depending on the selection criteria and various other factors. The fee structure is updated on the college website from time to time. 410 information, you can also see the prospectus.

Our academic resources include access to a wide variety of library materials, online learning resources, individualized academic counselling support, and a range of study support services as well as well-equipped classroom computers, high-speed internet connectivity, a hostel facility, etc. The infrastructure is backed strongly by knowledgeable faculties and sympathetic management.

Excellence in teaching and learning, ensuring that education is relevant to societal demands, fostering peer collaboration and mentoring, embracing diversity, and promoting respect for all people are the basic values of the M.R. College of Education.
